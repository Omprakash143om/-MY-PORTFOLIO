About Me

Hello! I'm Anantha Omprakash, a B.Tech student in Computer Science and Technology at Madanapalle Institute of Technology & Science, Madanapalle. My academic journey has been marked by a strong commitment to learning and growth, as reflected in my CGPA of 8.77. I graduated from Sri Siddartha Junior College, Madanapalle, with a remarkable score of 96.2% and completed my S.S.C from Sri Chaitanya English Medium High School with a 9.8 GPA.

My passion lies in the fields of Python programming, data science, and database management systems. I have undertaken multiple internships, including a data science internship at HDLC Technologies, where I worked on the classification of datasets using KNN and SVM algorithms. Additionally, I gained practical experience in Python programming during my internship at YBI Foundation, and I developed skills in WordPress development during my time at Infinity Spark India Pvt Ltd.

Beyond academics, I am keenly interested in various areas, including web development, where I have experience using WordPress and Figma. My technical skill set includes languages such as Python, SQL, and HTML, with a strong foundation in database management with MySQL.

I am always eager to learn and grow, constantly seeking new challenges and opportunities to enhance my skills. Please feel free to reach out to me through the Contact page or connect with me on social media.
